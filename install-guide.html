First-Time Setup Steps

1

Administrator Authorization

When you first open the app, it will ask for the Administrator Password. This is set by LKC Accounting and is required before anyone can install and configure the software. This protects against unauthorized distribution.

2

Business Profile Setup

Enter your business name, type, currency, contact, and address. These details appear on all your invoices and PDF reports automatically.

3

Set Your Access PIN

Choose a 4-digit PIN that only you know. This PIN will be required every time you open the app, keeping your business data secure.

4

Start Using the App

Begin recording income, expenses, stock items, and creating invoices. Everything saves locally on your device — no internet needed for daily use.

Administrator Password (LKC Owner)

The default admin password is: LKC@Admin2024
You can update this in the app's data before distributing to clients. Each client installs and sets up their own business profile and PIN — the admin password prevents unauthorized redistribution.

Important: The administrator password is not the same as the daily PIN. Do not share the admin password with clients. Each client sets their own PIN during setup.

Features Included

Income & Expense Tracking

Record, edit, delete, and filter all financial transactions with categories and payment methods.

Profit & Loss Reports

View P&L by this month, last month, this year, or a custom date range with category breakdowns.

Invoice & Receipts

Generate professional invoices with your business name, line items, tax, discount, and payment status.

Stock / Inventory

Track unlimited products with quantities, pricing, and reorder levels. Deducts stock on every sale.

Get instant alerts when any item drops below your set threshold. Badge shows count on the menu.

PDF Downloads

Download any report or invoice as a PDF when connected to internet. Works on all devices.

PIN Lock Screen

4-digit PIN protects your data. Auto-locks when you leave the app. Change PIN anytime in settings.

Offline First

All data saved locally. Works completely without internet. PDF download requires internet only.
